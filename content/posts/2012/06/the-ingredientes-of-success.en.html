--- title: "The ingredientes of the success" date: 2012-06-15T18:39:30+00:00 slug: the-ingredientes-of-the-success categories: [uncategorized] ---
  • Two litres of water
  • 5 or 6 large spoonfuls of sugar
  • Salt
  • Some lemon juice
  • 9 eggs
  • Half glass of water
  • 1 kg. of flour
  • Fat (to grease the frying pan)

As you put them out of the frying pan, you spread each one with anisette using a small brush and sprinkle with some sugar.
